Version v1.0
Live 2026 · 05

Initial launch.

The first public version of PlantAI. A complete plant-care companion in your pocket: AI species identification, GPT-4o health analysis, watering reminders, and a private health timeline for every plant.

The core app.

A five-screen experience that holds everything you need to identify, diagnose, and care for your plants.

Five-screen experience

Dashboard, Plants, AI Check, Alerts, and Settings. Tab-bar navigation built for one-handed use.

Dashboard

At-a-glance overview of every plant: care due today, recent health checks, and any alerts that need attention.

Plants library

Your entire collection in one view, each with its health badge, species, and last check date.

Plant Profile screen

A full record per plant: identification result, watering schedule, care log, and a chronological photo timeline.

AI plant intelligence.

GPT-4o vision powers identification and health analysis. The AI looks at your photos the way an expert would.

GPT-4o species identification

Photograph any plant and PlantAI identifies the species in seconds, then suggests care defaults tuned to that species.

AI health analysis

Take an AI Check photo and the model reads leaves, stems, and colour to flag pests, diseases, and nutrient stress.

AI-calculated watering schedule

Watering frequency and volume are derived per species and adjusted for season, so you never guess at intervals.

Sunday AI weekly digest

Every Sunday at 6 pm, a short AI-generated summary of how your plants are doing arrives in the app.

Tracking & memory.

Everything that happens to your plant is remembered, indexed, and ready to review.

SQLite plant database

Plants, health checks, alerts, and care logs are stored locally per user, with full sync to the cloud.

Photo-based health timeline

Every photo you take is saved against the plant alongside AI notes, so you can scroll back through its history in seconds.

Watering tracker

Tap Log Watering when you water a plant. The schedule recalculates from the actual time, not the suggested time.

Camera & capture.

A custom camera designed for plant photography, not selfies.

Full-screen camera

Edge-to-edge viewfinder that gives you the largest possible canvas for framing leaves, stems, and whole plants.

Torch mode

Turn on the device flash for low-light captures so the AI can still read fine detail in shadow.

Gallery import

Pull existing photos straight from your camera roll if your plant isn't in front of you right now.

Notifications & alerts.

Gentle, useful pings. Nothing that interrupts you outside of when it matters.

Watering reminders

Push notifications when a plant is due for water, timed to your daylight hours, not midnight.

Check-in alerts

A nudge when it's time to run a health check on a plant you've been watching.

Quiet hours

Set a window when PlantAI will hold all notifications until morning.

Account & system.

A solid foundation for the rest of the product: Supabase auth, theme support, and a public support site.

Supabase email authentication

Email sign-up and sign-in, full user data isolation, and automatic session restore on app launch.

Dark and light mode

A complete theme system across every screen, with automatic switching when your device theme changes.

Public support site

The first version of officialplantai-app.github.io shipped with FAQ, contact, and the PlantAI Privacy Policy.
